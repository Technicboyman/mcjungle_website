Full Breakdown:

Dark Pants + Chunk of Vile

Dark Pants are a new type of pants with both new and returning enchants from the real Pit game. They are centered around mitigating and countering mystics. They can be enchanted in the Mystic Well for a hefty gold price!

Chunk of Vile is the hottest new item added to MCJungle with many uses including repairing and of course creating the new Dark Pants. To obtain them, complete Night Quests, streak using Night Stalker, and complete contracts.

Rage Pants

Rage Pants are a new type of mystic pants with a both new and returning enchants from the real Pit game. You can obtain rage pants by unlocking Pure Rage in the renown shop and spending 15x Chunk of Vile, and they can be enchanted like a normal mystic, except that they will gain a new RAGE enchantment for tier 1!

Mystic Repairing

Mystic Repairing is a long requested feature that finally is coming to The Pit. Mystics can be repaired using Chunks of Vile along with the Withercrafting killstreak. Every 25 kills, a set amount of vile will be removed from your inventory and the item in your mystic well will get repaired 1 life.

New Map Areas

Multiple new areas have been created for players to gain new rewards and engage in new activities.

  • Underground Forest: The Underground Forest is a new area where players can chop down trees and sell logs to the Foraging Trader to gain cool items like vile and Jewel Swords. Additionally, chopping logs will give you extra points for the Snow vs Sand quest!
  • Fantasy Underground Revamp: The old strip mining underground area has been replaced with a fixed cave system with regenerating ores and a new mechanic: Shiny Blocks. Shiny blocks can be mined to give additional ores, xp, gold, and maybe even a Jewel Bow.
Colosseum

The Colosseum is a new area in the Snow vs Sand map at the back of the Dunes area. This area allows for both casual and competitive PVP to occur.

There are 2 colosseum modes: FFA and Duel, and they get rotated every hour.

In FFA mode, anyone is free to enter the arena and PVP with others, and while inside the arena, no lives are lost. Perfect for friendly godfights and duels.

In Duel Mode, you may use the /duel command to duel another player for a gold wager where the winner collects all the prize money and the loser walks out with nothing. No lives are also lost in this mode.

Hunting Bots

Hunting Bots are a new addition to The Pit. They have a 0.5% chance of spawning from kills over 400 streak. Hunting bots will only target you, pathfinding and sprint jump to your location and attacking you. There are 4 types that can spawn

  • Normal Hunting Bot - 3/4 diamond + diamond sword
  • Bounty Hunter Bot - Deals extra damage to bountied
  • Hacker Hunter Bot - Extended reach
  • Fling Hunter Bot - Sword deals extra knockback

Upon killing a hunter bot you will be rewarded with rewards such as:

  • 100 kills worth of gold
  • 100 kills worth of XP
  • Renown
  • Vile
  • Extra damage for a bit
New Player Lobby

Players under prestige 10 by default will now spawn in a lobby only accessible by them, improving the new player experience.

Player Reporting

An in-game report command has been added. Reporting a player in The Pit will result in a staff alert and the next 5 minutes of their gameplay will be captured in a replay for staff to view later.
