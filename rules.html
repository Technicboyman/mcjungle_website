MCJungle Rules and Punishments

Of course like any other server, MCJungle has it's own rule set to ensure fair play and community positivity. Each subcategory will go over what the rule is and also the ban lengths for them. Note that the ban lengths are calculated based off your TOTAL bans, not per-category. So for example if you get banned for cheating and then for autogrinding the autogrinding ban will be considered a second offence ban!

How bans are issued on MCJungle: Video clips submitted by players, replays, and staff members watching your gameplay or chat activity. Note that staff may not show ban evidence to the offender due to privacy and security concerns.

What can you do to help? Upon seeing a cheater or any rule breaker, use the command /report on them for the reason 'cheating' or 'boosting' and a replay of their upcoming activity will be captured and viewed by a staff member later. Additionally, recording the offender breaking the rules can get them banned.

1. Cheating (Punishment Lengths: 1d/3d/7d/14d)

The rules for cheating on MCJungle are as follows:

- Autoclicker: Allowed
- Aimassist: Allowed
- Killaura: Allowed (1 target + 3.0 reach)
- Movement Hacks (noslow, fly, etc): DISALLOWED
- Autoblock: DISALLOWED
- Velocity/AntiKB: DISALLOWED
- Reach: DISALLOWED
- Anything else: DISALLOWED


2. Autogrinding/Boosting (Punishment Lengths: 3d/7d/14d/28d)

The definition of Autogrinding on MCJungle is as follows:

- Any form of automatic attacking is autogrinding
- Any form of automatic movement is autogrinding
- Bans will be issued if you fail an autogrinding check such as a captcha

What is considered as boosting on MCJungle is:

- Autogrinding in The Pit game
- Using exogenous bots or alt accounts to boost stats
- Exploiting specific bugs that result in fast progression


3. Bug Exploiting (Punishment Lengths: 3d/7d/14d/28d)

What is considered bug exploiting on MCJungle is:

- Using server-crashing exploits
- Taking advantage of bugs that cause major harm to other players

Note: Minor bugs or unintended 'features' being abused is not punishable, for example, the exploit Tank Perk + Shadow enchant to gain permanent speed 2

Note 2: Item duping has it's own special category


4. Item Duping (Punishment Length: Permanent/Special)

Anyone caught duping will be subjected to an immediate permanent ban.
BUT the ban may be lifted or reduced based on the following conditions:

- If you decide to report the dupe glitch
- The scale of the dupe (how much was duped)


5. Threats (Punishment Lengths: 3d/7d/14d/28d)

What are considered as threats on MCJungle is:

- Real life threats of personal attack on someone including threatening to hire a hitman, visit their location, harm them, or ddos them
- Other extreme forms of threats and toxic behaviour


6. Doxxing (Punishment Lengths: 28d/90d)

Doxxing is a serious offence on MCJungle. What is considered as doxxing on MCJungle is:

- Information revealed about another user or their family including their location, full name, email, and more.

Note: If the 'dox victim' is okay with certain forms of doxxing like calling them by their real name it is not punishable. Also, light forms of doxxing will not be treated as a full dox.


7. Scamming (Punishment Length: Permanent)

What is considered as scamming on MCJungle is:

- Not holding up your side of the trade deal, such as not paying items after a trade agreement
- Drop sniping an item during a drop trade
- Picking up someone's accidently dropped gear and refusing to give it back

Note: If the user decides to return the items or hold up their end of the trade agreement they will not be punished. If they don't they will be permamently banned and the scammed items will be manually removed from the scammer's account or the victim will be refunded.


8. Chat Rules

Chat rules and standardized mutes are coming soon but for now you may take advantage of some new MCJungle features to mitigate chat toxicity:

- The ingame chat filter (/chatfilter to toggle)
- /ignore add (to not see a player's messages)
