About MCJungle

MCJungle is a Pit remake that aims to stay faithful to the original Hypixel Pit game but remains innovative.



Experience mystic well enchanting, accelerated progression, new maps, quests, and events! MCJungle has it all. Additionally, have your voice be heard as MCJungle prides itself as a community-driven game, where users can suggest ideas that might actually get added for once instead of ignored!
